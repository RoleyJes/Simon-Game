Simon(model) Game

How to Play

Put your memory to the test!

Press Any Key on Your Keyboard to Start

How to Play

  • Press any button on your keyboard.
  • Click on the button that is flashed.
  • To pass level 2, click on the previous flashed button, then the newly flashed button.
  • To pass level 3, click the previous flashed buttons in the order of the above step before clicking the newly flashed button.
  • Repeat the above step for subsequent levels
